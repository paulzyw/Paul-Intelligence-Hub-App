# Paul Wang Portfolio: The Digital Journey & Collaboration Log

This document serves as a "narrative archive" of the collaboration between Paul Wang and his AI Engineering Partner. It captures the evolution of the platform from a conceptual portfolio to a high-end industrial impact dashboard.

## Phase 1: The Foundation (Vision & Logic)
**Focus:** Establishing the "Commercial Edge" identity.
- **Key Dialogue:** We discussed the need to move beyond a static CV. The goal was to create a platform that demonstrated *leadership through data*.
- **Decisions:** 
    - Selected **Supabase** for a serverless yet robust PostgreSQL backbone.
    - Chose **Tailwind CSS 4.0** (cutting-edge) to ensure the site's architecture remained modern for years.
    - Implemented **Meteor Backgrounds** and **Glassmorphism** to reflect a "High-Tech Industrial" aesthetic.

## Phase 2: Structural Integrity (Data & Scalability)
**Focus:** Bridging the gap between the frontend and real-world data.
- **Key Dialogue:** Discussions around the **Insights** engine and how to manage thought leadership articles without a bulky CMS.
- **Decisions:**
    - Custom integration with **MailerLite** via Supabase Edge Functions to handle newsletter subscriptions securely.
    - Implementation of **Industrial Research** portals, allowing for whitepaper distribution.
    - Centralized formatting logic (`formatCurrency`, `formatNumber`) to ensure cross-page consistency.

## Phase 3: The "Value Engine" (Storytelling through Motion)
**Focus:** Visualizing the invisible impact of digital transformation.
- **Key Dialogue:** "How can we show value, not just list it?" This led to the creation of the *Value Delivery* section.
- **Decisions:**
    - **Directional Motion:** Strategy/Employer logic enters from the Left; Market/Customer impact from the Right.
    - **Viewport-Aware Count-Up:** Metrics only animate when the user views them, creating a "progressive reveal" effect.
    - **Precision UI:** Refining metrics from raw numbers to human-readable formats (e.g., $627M, 10.3M Tons).

## Phase 4: Maintenance & Domain Evolution
**Focus:** Preparing the app for its public launch.
- **Key Dialogue:** Navigating domain name changes and the security of API tokens (MailerLite/Supabase).
- **Decisions:**
    - Transitioned to **Supabase Secrets** management for the MailerLite API to keep keys out of the public browser.
    - Updated the **`DOCUMENTATION.md`** to act as a hand-over guide for future maintenance.

## Collaborative Philosophy
Throughout this journey, our dialogue focused on **Intentional Design**. Every animation, metric, and data point was designed to reinforce Paul's role as a leader who understands both the **Strategy** (Internal) and the **Impact** (External). 

This website is not just a digital resume; it is a functioning engine that demonstrates the very principles of digital transformation it discusses.
